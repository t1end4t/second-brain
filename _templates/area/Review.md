# {Area Name} — Review

## Daily

- Review unfinished `Today` and `Now` items.
- Pick the next useful action.
- Move blocked work to `Waiting`.
- Move completed work to `Done` with the date.

- Review what changed this week.
- Remove or move stale tasks.
- Identify one or two outcomes for next week.
- Update `Overview.md` if the direction changed.

- What is still important here?
- What is blocked?
- What should be stopped or archived?
