# AGENTS.md

Reading area for paper queues, research keywords, and notes.

Read first:
- `Overview.md` for scope.
- `TODO.md` for active reading tasks.
- `ICLR-2026-reading-list.md` for the current ICLR 2026 queue.

Conventions:
- Keep paper lists deduped by OpenReview URL.
- Use `[ ]` for unread and `[x]` for read/keep.
- Do not recreate `*-candidates.md` files unless explicitly asked.
- Use `paper-note-template.md` when making individual paper notes.
