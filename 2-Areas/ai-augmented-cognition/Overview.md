**Status:** Active

## What This Area Is

Exploring how humans learn and work effectively alongside LLMs. Not about productivity hacks — about preserving and amplifying human cognition in the age of instant answers.

## Core Thesis

> People who use LLMs as "answer machines" get weaker.
> People who use LLMs as "thinking partners" get stronger.

The advantage shifts from **knowledge** to **judgment + taste + problem framing + execution**.

## Why This Is an Area (Not a Project)

There is no end date. The landscape keeps changing. This is a continuous practice of:

- Developing personal AI interaction patterns
- Maintaining independent thinking capacity
- Building scientific taste alongside implementation skill
