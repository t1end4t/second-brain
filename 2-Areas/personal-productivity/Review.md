# Personal Productivity — Review

## Daily Startup

1. Capture loose thoughts first.
2. Review yesterday's completed work, blockers, and unfinished tasks.
3. Check active project `TODO.md` files.
4. Check relevant area `TODO.md` files.
5. Choose one main outcome for today.
6. Choose two or three secondary actions.
7. Update the relevant `TODO.md` files.

## Monday Weekly Review

1. Review last week's done work.
2. Review missed or stale commitments.
3. Check all active projects in `1-Projects/`.
4. Check ongoing areas in `2-Areas/`.
5. Decide this week's outcomes.
6. Move tasks to `Now`, `Next`, `Waiting`, `Later`, or `Done`.

## End-of-Day Closeout

1. Move completed work to `Done` with today's date.
2. Record blockers in `Waiting`.
3. Add important context to the relevant project or area note.
4. Leave one clear next action for tomorrow.

## Priority Buckets

- `Urgent`: deadline or consequence soon.
- `Important`: moves a project or life direction forward.
- `Needed`: maintenance, admin, health, setup.
- `Waiting`: blocked by someone or something else.
- `Later`: valid but not current.
